### Misc work on the cowling

#### Exhaust pipe cover

I fitted a shell (cover?) for the exhaust pipe so it's more aerodynamic. The process is pretty simple. I used some blue painter tape to temporarily secure the cover, then drilled 5 tiny holes for 3/32 rivets. Then I just used a debur tool to aggressively clear the drill holes. The holes are enlarged slightly and also countersunk. Then I shot 2.4mm countersink rivets to attach the cover to the lower cowl permanently.

#### Exahust pipe clamp

Another thing is that when I mounted the lower cowl I noticed the clamp bolt I installed yesterday on the exhaust touches the lower cowl.

So I removed the nut on the clamp, and flipped the bolt 180 degrees and re-installed it. All is clear.
