## Details

### Assembled the propeller hub

Last time I already put the hub together. But when I connected them I did not put loctite on the screws.

I took it apart and put some loctite 222 on the small screws, and then put it back together.

And I also connected the wires from the hub to the hub's brush flange.

### Fitting on the engine flange

After the hub is fully assembled, I took it to the engine flange to fit it on. I then hand tightened the 6 bolts to put the hub in place. The bolts went in very easily.
