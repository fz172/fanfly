## TLDR

- Replaced the factory exhaust pipe with the Sling custom version

## Details

It was a long day cutting and fitting the exhaust pipe. Some heavy tools were used and it was nerve wrecking cutting into a perfectly new engine. But it had to be done.

I used Patrick Shine's [blog](https://sling4.jetshine.net/2023/03/27/exhaust-pipe/) as the blueprint and pretty much did exactly what he did.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczMpnYU9R0BWIt_-zOGiiZLURPfwyGcKkmzOrDt19K_x03QqI_veY56iab7QlDvBmY2bwFP_czwzl92K7ojKBhAbMwKjtq5qtlwLU_yCXua_uu05xS8gAoSZR3C2oZcbIhtHPDZroiUOZsPA1XQ_uWl8Hg=w1709-h1282-s-no-gm?authuser=0)
