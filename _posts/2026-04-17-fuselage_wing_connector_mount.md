## TLDR

- Installed custom mount for the wing connectors

## Details

During the Midwest Panel Builder's visit a couple of weeks ago, they offered to make me a custom mount to secure the wing connector on the fuselage side. That sounded like a great idea, so I took them up on the offer.

The parts arrived this week, so I put it on the airplane.

### Before the install

The parts came with 2 plates to be installed onto the fuselage, and mounts the wing connector onto the plates. And there is also a 3D printed jig to drill the holes for the plates. The whole design is quite elegant.

### Installation

With the jig, I drilled the holes on the fuselage and test fitted the mounting plates.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczMeXw2f3tTHqKchUo3_7EyJ-UiV0WbaLQJLdYB9P4cU4NF55aeFwhIl7SfJClKWjrC7m3dDyDWxV_Kv2lcB7MyWdrPJDRXIjvKnrWRuBlk1ZL018bry0q8yxnJvAbMMFs6JYjLCbP5uBkCCJUZnoXzc_w=w1159-h869-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

Then riveted the plates permanently in place with 4.0mm rivets.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczMk0yPgeZgo9quNTq6I8fvBpW4Nq96dYTe432FF81C11WQMBX52hUWaKIaNrYIbHtUJrEiEVMokNQDvSD7G9JWOJo-Z0vH85X29zsOtBOe3RaxqwG3B9iEq46EX5n_Id1_njhRHBIBQmUO3abUfKOYaaw=w1159-h869-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

The connetor itself is mounted on the plate through 4 M4 screws and locknuts on the back.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczMCwi-X4_TwYFwL2gs8Tat8uwuBOYaLga-yXwTe3R8y1Co-4fAwkoONEeKwTbn5Y376SJ6aQ8f9Cb6efc4jXrvdgbfNBQ2Yc7-rr0TPTJsFjgX1pasepk1rqBbV-TkNdrBbLo1jo8bWSTSkCFiJcRcy3Q=w1159-h869-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

As a note, I rotated the connectors so first pin is at the top. I will remember this when connecting the wing harness wires.
