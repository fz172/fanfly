## Details

### Routed the throttle cable from the throttle quadrant to the engine.

Initially I wanted to use a metal cable guard through the firewall. But the space was too tight. I could not safely drill a hole for the cable guard. I had to gave up on this idea and switch to the Sling official way, which is to use a rubber grommet to protect the throttle when going through the firewall.
