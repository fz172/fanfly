## Details

### Cowing

I put the cowing 2 pieces pointing upwards, and aligned them to ensure the hole around spinner is connected together nicely. Then drilled holes 5.5mm away from the tip per instruction.

![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczNzbuYIqUoR6shKo8cdmz1QtMQnjoXt__VH4kvIAHTYlxWKbFKJIV0rf8r8Euz_KQ08xr7SExB9g1HW_95CgpWaWHB5_6-UIxmeDCLNOKG2A6CdL-orIpaUGfq8OCkBcAOEwTLoOjZnczdMD4VLHegPKQ=w629-h839-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczOpV5_w2emZzODLLQ60MUtBSJBnTPPiF-hplnE1akXre0HskecyNvok8ntEJTssSR8DA-FegRdAzL73hAE7Beq6z0Dke5YBMeWj6qDUvNeRV_dSn1QnoUTjMHAMQjmCVFxTl1DesgKscvESXnf5GZKTMA=w1119-h839-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

Then I lined up the rest of the cowling edge and drilled holes every 28.5 cm.

![img](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczPro9ouxFgtEixYxTSI5hj_IK-VVs9TpwWvlWBD4dsmThnkrN3X417Z5TCVMXDjEDv13C7wiB_ZOgoxQ2_-9elg9J2_9qR2Db1J00oZfXv3gIKuKGtYzQWaAknGefLGcC_rRQ3R4D-8cCwR0lqKybcOKg=w629-h839-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

Then I took a marker to highlight the areas that needed some sanding before removing the clecos. I used a 60 grits sanding block and a sanding disc on a dremel to give the edges some light sanding.

After a few rounds sanding and checking, the cowing fits together seamlessly.
