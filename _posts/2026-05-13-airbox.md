## TLDR

- Put together the air filter

## Details

The air filter lives right next to the intercooler. I decided to assemble it as a small task before the day ends.

The steps are pretty straight forward. I drilled a hole at the center of the filter, put a 6mm bolt through, then put the air filter into a plastic box and use another nut to hold the filter and box together.

The plastic box has 2 small holes for air sensor. I test fitted the sensors, and they should fit nice when the time comes.
