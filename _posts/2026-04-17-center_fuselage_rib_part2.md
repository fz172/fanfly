### Rear Fuselage Rib Replacement

Continuing from last week's work, I finished putting on the remaining rivets of the rear fuselage rib. Some holes did not align well, I used a 1/8 drill bit to clean up the holes and followed by thoroughly deburring them. The process took a while due to the access. It wasn't too bad, but still required some delicate movements to avoid stepping/breaking anything important.
